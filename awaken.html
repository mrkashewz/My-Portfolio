Awaken

Meditation App

Summary and Research

Summary

Awaken is a meditation app that allows users to set timed meditation sessions, learn how to meditate through guided videos as well as view and track their progress.

Problem

After conducting surveys as well as studying the competition in today’s meditation app market, I came up with few problems that I wanted to fix. The first , is that most users don’t have a steady meditation practice. On average most survey participants have meditated less than an hour in the past month. In addition, some users requested quotes by spiritual teachers in a meditation app, which isn’t currently found in the top apps such as Headspace or Insight Meditation. In addition, users would like a way to track their meditation progress which isn’t present in some meditation apps today.

Solution

To address the issue of infrequent meditation sessions, there will be a “Set Reminders” option that will be presented to users upon signing up. In addition, there will be quotes from spiritual teachers, as requested, in the Meditation Timer section. As for other features, the results from the survey indicated that users would prefer to hear audio cues in their meditation practice over kinesthetic and visual cues and would like guided meditations in a video format (as opposed to solely audio). I will implement these features in my project as well as create a page where users can track their meditation progress as requested.
